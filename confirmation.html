If you are a new customer:
We will call you to confirm in 10 minutes.

If you are a returning customer:
No telephone calls will be initiated by us, except if we need confirmation from you.

Please make sure your mobile phone is on and can receive calls. We will need to call you if:
- Estimated delivery time is more than 60 minutes.
- Your food is not available.
- We need additional info on your delivery address.

Order Cancellation:
All order cancellation has to be done through phone at 021 2954 5000.
All orders that have been confirmed by the restaurant cannot be cancelled.
Usually this is a 10 minutes window.
